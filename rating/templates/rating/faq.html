Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use this site?

RTB does not deal with services outside of boba tea. This is what makes it from other feedback aggregators like Google Maps or Yelp. We are solely focused on boba to focus on the needs of boba shop owners and boba enthusiasts alike.

What is boba tea?

Bubble tea is a Taiwanese tea-based drink invented in Tainan and Taichung in the 1980s. Recipes contain tea of some kind, flavors of milk, and sugar. Toppings, known as "pearls", such as chewy tapioca balls, popping boba, fruit jelly, grass jelly, agar jelly, alovera jelly, sago and puddings are often added.

Are you a public company?

Ratetehboba is a nonprofit organization started by boba lovers for boba lovers. We do not sell ads and have no revenue.
